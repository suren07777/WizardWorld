The Tale of the Three Brothers

Long ago, when three brothers came face to face with Death, he granted them three magical objects: an unbeatable wand, a stone that could call back the dead, and a cloak of invisibility. Yet, violence and foolish desire led two of the brothers to ruin. Only the youngest, the wisest of them, was able to trick Death for many years with the cloak.In the end, he passed the cloak down to his son and met Death as an old friend, departing peacefully. This tale speaks of wisdom, humility, and the true value of choice.
